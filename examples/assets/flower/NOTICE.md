(public-domain-dedication) photograph "flower" by imuzio, obtained via Openverse:
https://api.openverse.org/v1/images/16b88d7f-4185-438f-a976-03008eddf63b

CC0 1.0 imposes no conditions on use; credit is given here as a courtesy.
The image was center-cropped to square, resized, and sliced into a 6×6 patch grid
for the `autoregressive_flower.py` example.
